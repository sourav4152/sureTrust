Our Story

We founded our spiffy establishment in 2021 to offer a comfortable and fun place to connect with friends, new and old, over great food that anyone can enjoy.

Made Fresh

All of our crêpes and a selection of condiments and beverages are made fresh in-house.

Home Grown

We work with local farms and suppliers to provide our ingredients year-round whenever possible.

Upscale Comfort

More than just a restaurant. Find a comfortable seat for some coffee, or find a table to play games with your friends.

For Everybody

We offer a variety of crepes and options for those with dietary restrictions.
